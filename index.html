About the Anime

Naruto Shippuden follows Naruto Uzumaki’s journey as he grows stronger, faces powerful enemies like Akatsuki, and strives to become Hokage — the strongest ninja in the Hidden Leaf Village.

Main Characters

Naruto Uzumaki

The hero of the story, known for his determination and dream to become Hokage.

Power: Rasengan, Shadow Clone

Sasuke Uchiha

A skilled ninja driven by revenge and later redemption.

Power: Sharingan, Chidori

Sakura Haruno

A strong kunoichi with medical ninjutsu and incredible strength.

Power: Healing, Super Strength
